Facilities

Fireplace

The perfect place to gather and relax on cold winter evenings.

Communal Kitchen

Fully equipped with two stoves, two ovens, two microwave ovens and a multitude of kitchenware.

Shared Bathroom

One on each level per gender, with newly refurbished showers.

TV Lounge

Full 75 inches TV with Netflix, Amazon Prime and plenty of DVDs to keep you entertained for hours.

Balcony

Catch a picturesque view of the sunrise and sunset, or go stargazing on clear nights with the lodge's telescope.

Garden

Get some sunshines while reading books from the lodge's book exchange, or get grilling with the BBQ pits.

Amuri Avenue Reserve

Enjoy a 2 minutes walk down a stretch of oak trees on your way to the hot pools.
